ü™≥ What are DORA Metrics?

DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment) metrics are industry-standard measurements for software delivery performance. Like a cockroach that survives nuclear disasters, these metrics help you build systems that are resilient, fast-recovering, and nearly indestructible. The four key metrics are: Deployment Frequency, Lead Time for Changes, Mean Time to Recovery, and Change Failure Rate.
